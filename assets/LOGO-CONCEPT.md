# Bardacle Logo Concept

## Design Direction

**Theme**: Barnacle + Bard (memory keeper) + Crustacean family (OpenClaw heritage)

### Visual Elements

1. **Primary Shape**: Stylized barnacle shell
   - Conical/volcano shape typical of barnacles
   - Segmented plates (like real barnacles)
   - Opening at top

2. **Consciousness Indicator**: 
   - Soft glow or thought bubble emerging from the opening
   - Could contain a small spiral (representing memory/thought)
   - Or a simple dot pattern (like neural activity)

3. **Attachment**: 
   - Small "grip" lines at the base showing it attaches to things
   - Represents how Bardacle attaches to your agent

### Color Palette

**Primary**: 
- Deep ocean teal (#1a535c) - stability, depth
- Warm coral (#ff6b6b) - the glow of awareness

**Secondary**:
- Sand/shell (#f7fff7) - background
- Deep navy (#2c3e50) - text/outlines

### Recommended: Option A (Minimal)

For a developer tool, clean and minimal works best:
- Recognizable at small sizes (16x16 favicon)
- Looks good in dark and light mode
- Evokes the concept without being childish
- Easy to reproduce in ASCII for CLI
